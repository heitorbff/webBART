Instructions

The objective of this game is to get the largest number of points by pumping balloons.

You will see one balloon at a time on the screen, which you can pump as many times as you want, or stop whenever you want. Each pump adds 10 points to the balloon.

The more you pump, the more points you get. But the more you pump, the higher the chance that it will explode.

If a balloon explodes, you lose all points you were accumulating from it. Whereas if you stop pumping (clicking the button "Stop"), you accumulate all points you got from the balloon so far.

You can see on the top right corner of the screen how many points you got so far (that is, points you accumulated in previous rounds), how many you lost in previous rounds, and how many a competitor accumulated by the end of the current round.

Try to do better than the competitor!

After 12 rounds, the game is over. Come back in 2 weeks!
